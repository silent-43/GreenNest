Plant Care Tips 🌱

Watering Plants

Water your plants regularly, but avoid overwatering. Most indoor plants need water once a week.

Sunlight

Ensure your plants get proper sunlight. Some plants prefer indirect light, while others thrive in direct sunlight.

Soil & Fertilizer

Use nutrient-rich soil and appropriate fertilizer to keep your plants healthy and blooming.
